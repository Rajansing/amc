AMC Engineering College Highlights 2025

AMC Engineering College was established under the Paramahamsa Foundation Trust in 1999 and is ISO certified. The college is approved by AICTE, Government of India, and degree courses are affiliated with Bangalore University, while all engineering courses are affiliated with VTU (Visvesvaraya Technological University). The college is accredited by the NBA and NAAC. The college is ranked fourth among the Top Engineering Institutes in Bangalore by the Times All India Engineering Institutes Ranking Survey for 2023.

AMC College Bangalore offers UG and PG courses across various streams such as Engineering, Management, and others. The flagship course offered at AMC College is BTech. The institute offers scholarships to meritorious students. AMC Engineering College admission is based on merit and national entrance exams. The institute accepts national entrance exams such as JEE Main, CAT, XAT, GATE, and others. AMC Bangalore is spread over 52 acres of area which includes classrooms, a library, a cafeteria, a sports complex, a hostel, and others.

AMC Engineering College Admission Process 2025

AMC Engineering College admission is open. The college offers various Engineering, Science, and Management courses at the undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels. Admission to all the courses is subject to a valid score in the national/ state-level entrance exam along with the fulfilment of the minimum eligibility criteria set by the college. AMC Engineering College provides courses in affiliation with Visveswaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi. Students are offered the programmes in full-time and regular modes.

Admission Process Steps

  1. Application Process: Candidates must visit the official website and select the programme they want to apply to. The application process requires candidates to fill out the online application form with all the required details. To submit the form, students need to upload the relevant documents and certificates.
  2. Selection Process: The application forms will be scrutinised, and the selection will be made based on the eligibility criteria. Selected candidates will be invited for a counselling interview. Candidates can choose any options for the interview process, which are one-on-one, online, or telephonic. Additionally, candidates must also appear for the entrance exam, personal interview, and group discussion as per the programme requirements.
  3. Merit-list and Counselling: Wait for the merit list to be announced by the college. The merit list will be based on the entrance exam scores and other criteria such as cutoffs, academic performance, work experience, etc. Attend the counselling process and follow the guidelines provided by the college. The counselling process will involve document verification, seat allotment, fee payment, etc.
  4. Seat Offer: Once the application process and interviews are completed, the college admissions official sends an admission offer to the candidates. The candidate must accept the offer by confirming it and paying the course fees.
